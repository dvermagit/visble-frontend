## Visble - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 September 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Visble, operated by United Innovation Hub, collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information when you use our Services. By accessing or using Visble, you agree to this Policy.

**Personal Data:** Any information that can identify you, such as email, name, or IP address.  
**Usage Data:** Automatically collected information like device data, pages visited, time stamps, etc.  
**Services:** Visble’s websites, web apps, and related services for Generative Engine Optimization analytics.
